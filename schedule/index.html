Fall 2012 Upcoming Events

MU Tables

Hey there! We have a table at the MU every day for the week before and first week of school (from Monday Sept 24 to Friday Oct 5). See the calendar below for which table we'll be at each day. We'll be there at least from 11am-2pm, maybe before or after but no promises. Stop by and hang out with us! We'd be happy to answer any questions you have about Birdstrike or life in general.

Workshop

We're having a free, open to anyone improv jam from 7-9pm on Wednesday Oct 3rd. Haven't improvised before? Cool, we love introducing people to improv! The workshop will assume no prior improv knowledge, so come have fun with us, you won't be the only being introduced to improv. The workshop is also a great way to gain some practice for auditions.

Auditions

Soon Birdstrike Theatre will be having it's annual auditions! Come jam with us Thursday Oct 4 or Saturday Oct 13.
