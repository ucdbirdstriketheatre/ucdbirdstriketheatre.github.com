If you're wanting to learn about or see more improv, here are some good resources.

Books

Here are some books we like:

  • Truth In Comedy by Charna Halpern, Del Close and Kim Johnson.
  • Improvise: Scene From The Inside Out by Mick Napier.
  • The Improv Handbook: The Ultimate Guide to Improvising in Comedy, Theatre, and Beyond by Tom Salinsky and Deborah Frances-White.

Professional Improv

If you have the opportunity, we strongly recommend that you take iO, Upright Citizen's Brigade, or Second City classes. Reading books can give you many good ideas and an understanding of improv, but nothing beats live, professional instruction.

Friends

Birdstrike Theatre is in a loving but platonic relationship with a number of improv troupes, including:

  • Jericho at UC Berkeley.
  • Made Up Theatre in Fremont.
  • Awkward Silence at University of San Francisco.
